ARCADIA MUSICFEST 2023

Sheraton Parkway Hotel
RICHMOND HILL

Musicfest is Arcadia Academy's largest event, giving our students the opportunity to experience the joy of performing in front of their peers, friends and family, albeit, online this year. This competition has been and will always be a culmination and testimony of excellent teaching, support, and encouragement. This year, Arcadia Academy of Music is bringing Musicfest back as an in-person event! For more than three decades, we've had our competition bring all our locations together to celebrate and showcase the best and brightest our schools have to offer. For 2023, we wanted to make sure we continued that tradition, and after 2 years away, we can't be more excited to be back…live and in-person!
